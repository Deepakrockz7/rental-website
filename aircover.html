AirCover for Guests

Every booking comes with AirCover for guests. If there’s a serious issue with your stay that your Host can’t resolve, we’ll help you rebook or refund.

If anything comes up, your Host is your best point of contact. You can message your Host directly to let them know what’s going on.

Host cancellations

If your Host cancels within 30 days of check-in, we’ll help you find a similar place at comparable pricing. If a similar place isn’t available or you’d prefer not to rebook, we’ll issue a refund.

Learn more: What to expect if your Host cancels your reservation.

Unable to check in

If you can’t access your Airbnb and your Host doesn’t respond or can’t resolve the issue, we’ll help you rebook or refund, depending on availability.

See details: What to do if you can’t get in touch with your Host.

Inaccurate listings

If your listing is significantly different than advertised and your Host can’t resolve the issue, we’ll help you rebook or refund accordingly.

24-hour Safety Line

If you ever feel unsafe, you’ll get priority access to trained safety agents or local emergency authorities, day or night.

Need to reach us? Contact us by phone, email, or chat.

How AirCover for guests works

AirCover provides support for serious issues with your booking, such as Host cancellation within 30 days, inaccurate listings, or major amenity failures. It doesn’t cover minor inconveniences, like a broken toaster.

Your Host is the best first contact if anything comes up. Message your Host directly to let them know what’s going on.

  • Take photos or videos of the issue, if possible.
  • Contact your Host within 72 hours of discovering the issue.
  • If unresolved, contact us for rebooking or refund options.

Note: AirCover is not an insurance policy. It doesn’t cover unrelated travel issues (e.g., flight delays or lost luggage).
